Production

Extrusion Lines

At PRANSHI MULTIPLAST, we are proud to offer cutting-edge packaging solutions powered by the Kabra Multilayer Blown Film Extrusion Line — a globally trusted system for producing high-quality multilayer films. With this advanced technology, we ensure consistent performance, efficiency, and premium-quality output tailored to diverse industry needs.

About the Machine

The Kabra Multilayer Blown Film Line is a state-of-the-art extrusion system designed for producing multilayer films in 3, 5, and 7-layer configurations. These films are widely used in packaging applications across food, pharmaceuticals, industrial goods, and more.

Key Features

Our in-house Kabra line includes advanced features:
  • Auto Profile Thickness Control for uniform film layers
  • Gravimetric Dosing System for precise raw material feeding
  • Bubble Scanner for real-time monitoring and stability
  • ExtruTouch Control Panel for smart, user-friendly operations
This combination of automation and control delivers a high level of accuracy, efficiency, and film quality that meets international standards.

  • Multi-layer Extrusion
  • Automatic Thickness Control
  • High Production Capacity
  • Advanced Cooling System

6-Color Rotogravure Printing Machine

At Pranshi Multiplast, we believe in staying ahead of the curve through cutting-edge technology and world-class machinery. Our production facility is equipped with the 6-Color SHANTI SOLUTION High-Speed Rotogravure Printing Machine, ensuring unmatched quality, precision, and efficiency in every project we undertake.

Key Features of Our 6-Color SHANTI SOLUTION Printing Machine:

  • High-Speed Performance: Capable of delivering fast and flawless printing, our machine ensures quick turnaround times without compromising on quality.
  • 6-Color Precision Printing: Enables vivid, high-resolution multicolor printing with excellent registration accuracy—ideal for dynamic designs and branding.
  • Wide Substrate Compatibility: Prints efficiently on a variety of flexible packaging materials such as LDPE, HDPE, PP, BOPP, and more.
  • Automatic Tension Control & Web Alignment: Ensures seamless operations and reduces material wastage.
  • Eco-Friendly Ink System: Supports water-based and solvent-based inks, making our processes more sustainable and compliant with global standards.

How This Benefits You

  • Cost-Effective Production: Our efficient setup minimizes downtime and reduces waste, saving you money while maximizing output.

Slitting & Rewinding

At Pranshi Multiplast, we're committed to delivering not just great printing, but perfect finishing too. Our facility includes a high-performance Slitting Machine, designed to deliver sharp, accurate, and consistent cuts across a wide range of flexible packaging materials.

Key Features of Our Slitting Machine:

  • High Precision Cutting: Equipped with ultra-sharp blades and advanced tension control, our machine ensures clean, straight cuts without burrs or rough edges.
  • Adjustable Slitting Widths: Customizable settings allow for exact slitting to meet your product specifications—whether narrow rolls or wide formats.
  • High-Speed Operation: Supports fast slitting for large production batches while maintaining accuracy and consistency.
  • Automatic Rewinding System: Delivers tightly wound rolls with even edges—ready for immediate dispatch or further processing.
  • Low Waste Design: Engineered for optimal efficiency, minimizing waste and maximizing output.

How This Benefits You

  • Perfect Roll Sizes Every Time: Get rolls that are cut exactly to your needs, ready for packaging, sealing, or converting.
  • Professional Finish: Clean edges and precise alignment ensure a high-end, professional look for your packaging products.
  • Fast Turnaround: Our speed and precision help shorten lead times without compromising quality.
  • Versatile Applications: Ideal for BOPP, LDPE, HDPE, PET, laminated films, and other flexible substrates.
